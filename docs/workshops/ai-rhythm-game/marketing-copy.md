## Subhead

In this 90-minute offline workshop, junior high students use Airbotix Playground
to create an original rhythm dancing game with pop-concert energy, fantasy stage
battles, and arcade gameplay.

## Copyright-Safe Theme Language

Use:

- Pop-star monster-battle rhythm game.
- K-pop fantasy hero energy.
- Cinematic concert-stage battle.
- Shadow beat rivals.
- Original rhythm battle world.

Avoid:

- Official movie titles in public marketing.
- Netflix references.
- Character names.
- Song titles or lyrics.
- Screenshots, logos, costumes, or lookalike art.
